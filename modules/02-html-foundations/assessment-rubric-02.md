Criteria
1) Semantic content (Digital content creation)
- Emerging: inconsistent headings; generic links.
- Proficient: headings ordered; descriptive links.
- Exemplary: consistent structure; figure/figcaption used well.

2) Forms (Digital content creation)
- Emerging: labels missing/misaligned.
- Proficient: labels linked; required fields clear.
- Exemplary: helpful hints; keyboard accessible.

3) Tables (Information & data literacy)
- Emerging: unclear headers; alignment issues.
- Proficient: th/td used properly; readable layout.
- Exemplary: concise captions; no ambiguity.
